Our Teams

Personal Styling

The “secret sauce” that marries our data science with human stylists to create an individualized fix for each customer

The Personal Styling team is creating a user experience to allow our stylists to give our customers the best Fix they can.

Merchandising

Our buyers must manage a wide variety of styles, keeping our warehouses full so our data scientists can arm our stylists with something for every one of our customers.

The Merchandising team is building next-generation tools to keep our buyers and merchandise pipeline full of a wide variety of inventory to arm our stylists (and algorithms)

Warehouse & Logistics

Every day, hundreds of people swarm into our warehouses, making sure thousands of items get shipped to, and received back from, our happy customers.

Our Warehouse & Logistics team is crafting bespoke tools from laptops, barcode scanners, carts, and web-applications to make our warehouse associates efficient & effective.

Customer Experience

From scheduling a fix on their phone, to opening the box when it arrives, we want every interaction with a customer to feel special.

Our Customer Experience team is designing products and experiences to allow every customer to get the most out of Stitch Fix.
